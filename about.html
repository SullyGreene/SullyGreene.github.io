About Me

Hi, I'm Sully Greene, a passionate AI developer and tech enthusiast with nearly three decades of experience. My journey in technology began at the age of three, with my first computer being a Windows 95. Since then, I've grown deeply connected to computers, programming, and the ever-evolving world of tech.

At the age of 12, I ventured into the world of Linux, starting with Ubuntu and later exploring other flavors. Currently, Arch Linux is my daily driver for its customization capabilities, though I still enjoy using Windows 11 on my laptop for its intuitive interface. I've always had an innate curiosity for learning and solving challenges, which has driven my passion for AI and open-source projects.
